# Refund Policy

## Overview

Nirvana Cloud is committed to customer satisfaction. This document outlines our refund
policy for all subscription tiers.

## Monthly Plans

Monthly subscribers may request a full refund within **7 days** of the billing date.
After 7 days, no refunds are issued for the current billing cycle, but you may cancel
to prevent future charges.

## Annual Plans

Annual subscribers may request a **prorated refund** within **30 days** of purchase.
The prorated amount is calculated based on unused complete months remaining in the term.

## Enterprise Annual Plans

Enterprise customers on annual contracts are eligible for a **full refund within 14 days**
of contract signing. Between 14 and 90 days, a **prorated refund** is available, subject
to a 10% early-termination fee. Beyond 90 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis
by the Enterprise Success team.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email billing@nirvanacloud.io with your account ID and reason for the refund.
2. The billing team will respond within 2 business days.
3. Approved refunds are credited back within 5–10 business days.

## Non-Refundable Items

- Overage charges for compute and storage usage
- Add-on services consumed in full
- Reserved Instance commitments after the 7-day grace period

## Contact

Questions? Reach out to billing@nirvanacloud.io or open a ticket from your dashboard.
