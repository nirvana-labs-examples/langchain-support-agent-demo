# Support Escalation Policy

## Support Tiers

### Tier 1 — Community & Self-Service
- Access: All plans
- Channels: Documentation, community forum
- SLA: No guaranteed response time

### Tier 2 — Standard Support
- Access: Pro and Team plans
- Channels: Email, support portal
- SLA: First response within 8 business hours

### Tier 3 — Priority Support
- Access: Business plan and above
- Channels: Email, support portal, Slack Connect
- SLA: First response within 2 business hours, severity P1 within 30 minutes

### Tier 4 — Enterprise Dedicated Support
- Access: Enterprise plan only
- Channels: All Tier 3 + named CSM + phone hotline
- SLA: P1 response within 15 minutes, dedicated on-call engineer

## Escalation Criteria

A ticket is eligible for escalation when:

1. **P1 (Critical)**: Full production outage, data loss risk, or security incident.
2. **P2 (High)**: Significant performance degradation affecting > 20% of workloads.
3. **P3 (Medium)**: Feature unavailable, workaround exists.
4. **Repeat Issue**: Same problem has occurred 3+ times in 30 days.
5. **Customer Requested**: Enterprise customers may request escalation at any time.

## How to Escalate

1. In the support portal, set the ticket Priority to **P1** or **P2**.
2. For Enterprise customers, call the hotline: +1-800-NIRVANA.
3. The on-call engineer will be paged within 5 minutes of a P1 ticket being filed.

## Escalation Contacts

- Engineering On-Call: paged automatically via PagerDuty
- Customer Success Manager: assigned for all Enterprise accounts
- VP of Engineering: escalated by CSM for unresolved P1s > 4 hours
