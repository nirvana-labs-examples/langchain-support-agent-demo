# Billing FAQ

**Q: When am I charged?**
A: Monthly subscribers are charged on the same day each month (your billing anniversary date).
Annual subscribers are charged upfront for the full year.

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**
A: We retry the payment 3 times over 7 days. If all retries fail, your account is suspended
(not deleted). You have 14 days to update your payment method before data is purged.

**Q: Can I change my plan mid-cycle?**
A: Yes. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for startups?**
A: Yes — our Startup Program offers 50% off for 12 months for companies under 3 years old
with less than $1M ARR. Apply at nirvanacloud.io/startups.

**Q: What's included in the free tier?**
A: The free tier includes 1 shared-CPU VM (512 MB RAM), 10 GB storage, and 100 GB
outbound transfer per month. Free tier VMs are paused after 30 days of inactivity.

**Q: How do I get an invoice?**
A: Invoices are automatically generated and emailed after each payment. You can also
download them from Settings → Billing → Invoice History.

**Q: Can I get a discount for paying annually?**
A: Yes. Annual plans are billed at 10 months' cost, giving you 2 months free compared
to monthly billing.

**Q: What currencies do you accept?**
A: We accept USD, EUR, and GBP. All charges appear in your selected currency at checkout.
